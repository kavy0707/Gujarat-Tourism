Trimandir in Adalaj, near Gandhinagar, is a unique non-sectarian temple founded by spiritual leader Dada Bhagwan. It beautifully integrates Jainism, Shaivism, and Vaishnavism by housing idols of Lord Simandhar Swami, Lord Shiva, and Lord Vishnu under one roof.The temple complex offers a peaceful atmosphere, spiritual discourses,and meditation spaces,attracting devotees from different faiths.

Adalaj Stepwell, near Gandhinagar, is a 15th-century architectural marvel built by Queen Rudabai. Its Indo-Islamic design, detailed carvings, and historical significance make it a pop- -ular tourist attraction.
